Corona Infections

Rules

You have to infect people. You have a buget. Each infection costs something. When you infect a single person it will be cost 100 from your budget. When you want to infect a person and his or her neighbours it will be cost 200. If you want alsoo infect neighbours of the second level (neighbours of the neighbours) then you will pay 400 from your budget. When a person die he will disapear from the graph. Your goal to kill everybody with given budget.
